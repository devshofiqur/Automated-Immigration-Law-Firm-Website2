A flat-fee service means you pay one fixed price for everything listed in your package — no hourly billing, no surprise charges. The price you see is the price you pay, period. This includes all attorney time, form preparation, and support within your chosen tier.

Processing times vary by case type and USCIS workload. Family-based petitions typically take 8–18 months, while some employment visas can be processed in 3–6 months with premium processing. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your consultation.

In the Filing package, RFE response preparation is included at no extra charge. If you're on the Guidance package, RFE support can be added as a separate service. We'll help you build the strongest possible response to protect your case.

Absolutely. All client information is protected by attorney-client privilege and our strict privacy policy. We use bank-level encryption for all document uploads and never share your information with third parties without your consent.
